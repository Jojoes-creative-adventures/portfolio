  • About

    My full name is Joe Vercambre and I'm based in the south of France.

    I'm mostly used to working on logos and visual identity, editorial design and illustrations but I love going outside my comfort zone to learn new things.
    I'm passionate about typography and type design and believe that the form of a message can increase the impact of its meaning so I tend to focus my work around expressivity.

    If you think that approach could benefit your project, don't hesitate to contact me and tell me more about it.
