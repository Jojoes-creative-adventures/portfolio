Exeau CSE Report

2021 • Editiorial & layout design

For ExEau, a company specializing in water sanitation, integrating the principles of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is essential. The CSR report aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the actions taken and assess the company's maturity in CSR. This project required a clear and structured layout to effectively communicate information to stakeholders.


The design of this report is based on principles of readability and modernity. Each section of the report is highlighted by distinct colors and illustrative graphics. Key information is represented with infographics, linking content and form for greater clarity.


Report Structure

The report is divided into several sections, each dedicated to a particular aspect of CSR:

• Introduction: Use of images and column texts for a dynamic presentation of the company.

• CSR Context : Charts and diagrams to explain standards and reference frameworks.

• Stakeholders: Maps and diagrams to visualize interactions and relationships.

• Maturity Assessment: Comparative tables and bullet points for quick and effective reading of key information.

• Conclusion : With the data collected during the maturity assessment, the company rates the CSR strategy that is already in place and devise actions to improve it.


The layout of ExEau's CSR report was designed to be functional, allowing clear and concise communication of the company's CSR commitments and actions. This project illustrates the importance of a well-organized and structured presentation to enhance content and facilitate reader comprehension.
