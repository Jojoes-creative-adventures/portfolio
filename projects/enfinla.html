Finally here!

Poster

The best person to talk about it

Louisa

Actress and director

Working with Joe on the poster and flyers for Finally Here! was a smooth and enjoyable experience. Their active listening, reactivity, and creativity resulted in a visual that was absolutely faithful to my vision, with all the necessary information. Very professional work for a perfect result!

For Finally Here!, I designed a poster in close collaboration with the artist, in order to preserve her unique vision and style. The poster features the artist in four Polaroids. This simple and effective choice highlights the diversity of the characters and the energy of the show, while remaining faithful to its quirky and authentic spirit.

The flyers feature the poster on the front, with information for the public on the back. The Instagram story is an animated version of the poster, designed to catch the eye and spark interest on social media.

Thanks to this collaborative effort, Finally Here!’s visual style perfectly reflects the artist’s sense of humor and personality, while inviting the public to discover her lively and funny world. It’s a design that highlights her talent with ease and clarity.
