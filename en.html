About

I'm Joe Vercambre, freelance graphic designer under the sunny skies of Montpellier, south of France ☀️

I believe that every idea deserves to be heard and each vision deserve to be seen. Specializing in 360° branding, I am dedicated to turning your ideas into powerful and memorable visual identities. Whether you need an elegant layout, intuitive web design, a seamless user experience, or captivating illustrations, I am here to bring your ambitions to life.

Working with me means benefiting from a human and comprehensive approach. I strive to understand your values and goals in order to create designs that resonate with your audience. Together, we will highlight the unique personality of your project, maintaining open and honest communication throughout the process.

Ready to embark on this creative adventure? Contact me and let's create something meaningful together. ✨

The key steps in my process

Initial Contact

Every collaboration begins with an in-depth conversation about your project. Together, we will define the objectives and outline the scope to clearly identify your vision and expectations.

Quote

Following our initial discussion, I will provide you with a detailed and transparent quote. This will give you a clear picture of the costs and investment required to bring your project to life.

First Concepts

Time for creativity ! I'll show you the first drafts of your project. Together, we'll explore and adjust them to make sure they perfectly capture the essence of your vision.

Development

I then set about developing the final design. Once completed, I will present it to you for approval, ensuring that it meets all your expectations and targets.

Final Adjustments

After you have provided feedback on the final design, I will make any necessary adjustments. This step is crucial for refining every detail and ensuring a result that resonates and captivates.

Delivery and Support

Finally, I will deliver all the final files to you, along with user guides. My commitment does not end there: I remain available for any additional assistance, ensuring that your project continues to shine.
