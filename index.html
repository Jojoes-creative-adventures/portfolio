About

My full name is Joe Vercambre and I'm based in the south of France.

I'm working in both print and digital. This broad approach gives me the freedom to find the graphic solution best suited to the needs of each project.

I'm passionate about typography and type design. I believe that the shape of a message can increase the impact of its meaning so I tend to focus my work around expressivity.

If you think that approach could benefit your project, don't hesitate to contact me and tell me more about it.

What to expect when working with me?

OnBoarding call

We'll kick things off with a friendly conversation about your project. In this chat, we'll outline the goals and parameters, ensuring I fully grasp your vision and what you hope to accomplish.

Quote

Following our initial discussion, I'll prepare a detailed quotation. This document will break down the costs, giving you a clear picture of the investment required for your project.

First Concepts

Next up, I'll create and present the initial drafts. This is an exciting phase where we start to see your vision take shape. We'll go over these concepts together and make any necessary adjustments based on your feedback to ensure we're heading in the right direction.

Refinement

After refining the initial ideas, I'll work on developing the final design. I'll present this polished version to you for feedback, making sure it aligns perfectly with your expectations.

Final Tweaks

Once you've reviewed the final design, I'll make any last adjustments needed. This step is all about fine-tuning the details to ensure everything is just right.

Delivery & Support

Finally, I'll deliver all the final files along with guides on how to use them. I'm here to support you even after delivery, so feel free to reach out if you need any follow-up assistance.
